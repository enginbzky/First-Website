Canton Neuchâtel

The Republic and Canton of Neuchâtel is a French-speaking canton in western Switzerland. The capital is Neuchâtel. The only part of present-day Switzerland to enter the Confederation as a principality (in 1814), Neuchâtel has a unique history. Its first recorded ruler, Rudolph III of Burgundy, mentioned Neuchâtel in his will in 1032.
